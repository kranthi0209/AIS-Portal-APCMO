Step A — Download Pre-filled Template
The template is pre-filled with all active officers. Fill in Contribution Type (Department or District), Entity Name (department name or district name), and GSDP Score (0–100). Step B — Upload Completed CSV
Contribution Type must be exactly Department or District (case insensitive). For department-level officers, Entity Name is the department name. For district-level officers, Entity Name is the district name. GSDP Score must be a number between 0 and 100.
Contribution Types: 🏛️ Department — enter the department name in entity_name 📍 District — enter the district name in entity_name

identity_no REQ Officer's identity number — pre-filled in template officer_name REQ Full name — pre-filled in template contribution_type REQ Must be exactly Department or District (case insensitive) entity_name REQ Name of the Department (if type = Department) or the District (if type = District) gsdp_score REQ GSDP contribution score from 0 to 100 — higher means stronger contribution period_from REQ Period start date — pre-filled (YYYY-MM-DD), do not change period_to REQ Period end date — pre-filled (YYYY-MM-DD), do not change
